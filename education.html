Timeline of Education and Experience

2019

3 January Started second semester of MTech.

Started second semester of Master of Technology (MTech) in the Department of Systems and Control Engineering at IIT Bombay.

1 January Celebrated New year with Arnav.

Started working on this webpage with Arnav. It began with a series of commits on GitHub.

2018

16 July - 28 November Done first semester of MTech.

Completed the very first semester of my MTech at IIT Bombay. Time really flies!! Vacation started with a lot of apprehensions of CGPA.

1 June Published one book chapter.

One book chapter based on my work at IIT Mandi was published by Wiley-IEEE Press.

29 May Selected for ME program at BITS.

Selected for Master of Engineering (ME) in Embedded Systems at BITS Pilani. This was my second selection in a Master program after IIT Bombay.

23 April Published one onference paper.

One conference paper based on our 2015 internship at IIT Bombay was added to IEEE Xplore. It took a span of three years to finish the paper.

2017

1 May Joined IIT Bombay.

Joined FOSSEE at IIT Bombay to work as a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Kannan Moudgalya.

26 April Left IIT Mandi.

Left IIT Mandi with a heavy heart and a bag full of learning.

8 April Took part in Kalam Innovation Award.

Presented our work on the development of early warning system for landslides. This was shortlisted for the Kalam Innovation Award held at Sai Ram Engineering College in Chennai.

2016

15 December Joined IIT Mandi.

Joined IIT Mandi to work as a project associate under the supervision of Dr. Varun Dutt.

1 July - 31 August Interned in Taiwan.

Completed my first foreign internship TIGP-IIP. Worked in the group Social Networks and Human Centered Computing (SNHCC) under the supervision of Dr. Ronald Y. Chang at Academia Sinica Taiwan.

31 May Completed BTech at Jammu.

Completed first professional degree of my career. Became a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics & Communication Engineering from Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University Katra, Jammu & Kashmir.

15 May - 7 July Interned at IIT Bombay.

Volunteered for Scilab-Arduino workshop conducted by FOSSEE at IIT Bombay. About 150 participants (including faculties and students) had attended the workshop.
